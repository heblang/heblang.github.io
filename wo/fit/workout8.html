Fit - Workout 8

STRENGTHENING BACK MUSCLES, LOWER BACK, BACK OF THE HAMSTRINGS, GLUTES, ABS

3 sets of 15-20 repetitions, slow and concentrated (3-4 seconds per repetition). Between exercises (back - abs), perform light stretching. At the end of each workout, make sure to stretch.

First Exercise: Lie down with arms along the body, legs against the wall at a 90-degree angle. Keep the head, neck, and shoulders stationary. Alternate lifting legs off the wall with a pause at the maximum position.

Second Exercise: Use a makeshift item (like a towel). Hands slightly wider than shoulders, towel stretched, one hand on the floor, and the other raised upward. Simultaneously, lift the opposite leg, keeping the torso slightly lifted.

Stretching

Stretching 1. (using a makeshift item like a towel) Place a cushion between your back and the wall. Hands slightly wider than shoulders, towel stretched, pressing against the wall without changing the position of the torso. Repeat 15-30 times, slow and concentrated (shoulder joint mobility, chest).

Stretching 2. 2 sets, lying on your back, using chairs. Sequentially stretch the chest muscles at different angles (hold for 30 seconds). Hold a weight (0.5-liter bottle) in your hand, and so on.

Abs and Core

For abs, perform based on sensation, 3 sets (e.g., inhale-exhale once, inhale-exhale twice, and so on), for as long as comfortable, preferably no less than 15 times. Touch the floor only with the toes.
