Fit - Workout 7

STRENGTHENING BACK MUSCLES, LOWER BACK, HIP, GLUTES, ABS

3 sets of 15-20 repetitions, slow and concentrated (3-4 seconds per repetition). Between exercises (back - abs), perform light stretching. At the end of each workout, make sure to stretch.

First Exercise: Lie down with arms along the body, knees bent. Keep the head and shoulders stationary during the torso lift, with a pause at the top, focusing on the target muscle group.

Second Exercise: Use a makeshift item (like a towel). Arms perpendicular to the body, leaning against a wall, knees bent and resting on an elevation (towel). Rotate the legs towards the wall without touching the floor. The torso should remain stationary.

Stretching

Stretching 1. Maintain a straight torso, supporting leg bent at a 90-degree angle, the other leg extended forward, toes pulled toward you. Bend at a short amplitude, touching the floor with your hands (targeting the back of the thigh and calf).

Stretching 2. 2 sets, lying on your back, bend one leg and stretch the opposite arm, pulling without lifting the shoulder off the floor (lower back, front of the thigh). Hold for 30-60 seconds.

Abs and Core

For abs, perform based on sensation, 3 sets (e.g., inhale-exhale once, inhale-exhale twice, and so on), for as long as comfortable, preferably no less than 15 times. A safe and easy variation is the "plank."
