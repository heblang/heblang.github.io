Fit - Workout 12

MUSCLE STRENGTHENING: BACK, ABS, HAMSTRINGS

3 sets of 15-20 repetitions, slow and concentrated (3-4 seconds per repetition). Between exercises (back - abs), perform light stretching.

First Exercise: Lie on your stomach, lift one arm and the opposite leg alternately (left arm - right leg) for each set, for 15-20 seconds.

Second Exercise: (Using two chairs) Lie on one chair with bent knees, using the other for support with hands. Lift both legs first in a BENT POSITION and then fully extend. (Previously there was a variation of this exercise, but only with straight legs).

Stretching

Stretching 1. 2 sets, lie on your back, bend one leg, and use the opposite hand to create tension without lifting the shoulder off the floor (lower back, front surface of the thigh). Hold for 30-60 seconds.

Stretching 2. Starting on your knees, lower your hands to the floor and straighten one leg (standing on the toes). Try to transition to the elbows and stretch forward and backward, switching legs. Hold for 30 seconds (back and front surface of the thigh).

Abs and Core

For abs, perform based on sensation, 3 sets (exhale at the top) for as long as comfortable, preferably no less than 15 times. Keep the head slightly lifted (avoid excessive lifting), arms along the body, and touch the shins alternately with the palms.
