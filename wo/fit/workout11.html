Fit - Workout 11

STRENGTHENING BACK MUSCLES, LOWER BACK, GLUTES, ABS

3 sets of 15-20 repetitions, slow and concentrated (3-4 seconds per repetition). Between exercises (back - abs), perform light stretching.

First Exercise: Lie down with head, shoulders, and arms pressed to the floor, legs bent at a 90-degree angle. Lift the hips upward with a pause, lower down, and repeat. Perform without lifting the shoulders, head, and arms.

Second Exercise: Kneel with hands straight on the floor, try to touch the knee with the head while rounding and contracting the back. Then, move the same leg backward and upward.

Stretching

Stretching 1. 2 sets for each leg, hold for 4-5 seconds. Legs against the wall, lift the hips, bring one leg to the supporting leg, and with hands, pull the toes towards you while pushing the knee away. Lower the hips while counting to 4, then switch legs (glute muscles).

Stretching 2. Use a physio ball or tennis ball. Stand with one foot on the ball, using a chair for support, and roll the ball under the foot, transferring body weight onto the ball (relaxation of the arch of the foot muscles).

Abs and Core

For abs, perform based on sensation, 3 sets (exhale at the top), for as long as comfortable, preferably no less than 15 times. Consider adding a plank with leg lifts.
