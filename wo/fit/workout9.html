Fit - Workout 9

STRENGTHENING BACK MUSCLES, LOWER BACK, HIP, ABS

3 sets of 15-20 repetitions, slow and concentrated (3-4 seconds per repetition). Between exercises (back - abs), perform light stretching. At the end of each workout, make sure to stretch.

First Exercise: Lie down with arms along the body, legs on the floor. Head, neck, and shoulders remain stationary. Lift legs one at a time with the pelvis, holding at the maximum position.

Second Exercise: Use a makeshift item (like bottles). While lying down, extend arms forward. Lift the torso, retracting the shoulder blades and bringing the arms behind, then lower the torso with arms bending at the elbows. Lift the torso again and return to the starting position.

Stretching

Stretching 1. Perform at different angles with a 20-30 second hold in each position. Do it on each side, changing legs. (targeting the gluteus medius).

Stretching 2. 2 sets for each side, 30 seconds with a pause at the maximum position (hip joint mobility).

Abs and Core

For abs, perform based on sensation, 3 sets (e.g., inhale-exhale once, inhale-exhale twice, and so on), for as long as comfortable, preferably no less than 15 times. Maintain lumbar and pelvis in one line, head lifted to the left, arms stretched upward, knees bent at a 90-degree angle. Hold the static position.
