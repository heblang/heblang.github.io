Fit - Workout 2

STRENGTHENING BACK MUSCLES, LOWER BACK, GLUTES + ABS

3 sets of 15-20 repetitions, slow and concentrated (3-4 seconds per repetition). Between exercises (back - abs), perform light stretching. At the end of each workout, make sure to stretch.

While performing back exercises, the torso is slightly lifted above the surface, creating tension in the back muscles.

In the first exercise, with hands raised and elbows bent, when lifting the torso, bring the arms and shoulder blades back.

For the second exercise (using bottles), lie on your stomach with arms extended forward and legs together. Place bottles on either side of the legs around the shins. Without lifting the torso, raise one leg, avoiding contact with the bottle, and return to the starting position.

Stretching

Stretching 1. Stretching the abdominal muscles. Lie on your stomach. With palms on the floor slightly beyond the head, lift the torso while keeping the pelvis down. Take deep breaths (you should feel a stretch in the abdominal muscles from the groin to the upper ribs) and exhale deeply. Repeat at least 20 times. Rest for 30 seconds between sets.

Stretching 2. Lie on your back, using chairs. Sequentially stretch the chest muscles at different angles (hold for 30 seconds). Hold a weight (0.5-liter bottle) in your hand, and so on.

Abs and Core

Perform abs exercises based on sensation, 3 sets (exhale at the top), preferably no less than 15 times.

For abs, avoid lifting the neck strongly towards the chest; let the hands slide along the legs. A more challenging variation is to keep the arms in front.
