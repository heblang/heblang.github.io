Fit - Workout 6

STRENGTHENING BACK MUSCLES, LOWER BACK, ABS, PEAR-SHAPED MUSCLE, HIP

3 sets of 15-20 repetitions, slow and concentrated (3-4 seconds per repetition). Between exercises (back - abs), perform light stretching. At the end of each workout, make sure to stretch.

First Exercise: Rotate legs and head in opposite directions, keeping the legs off the floor in all positions to create tension.

Second Exercise: Use a makeshift item (like a towel). Keep legs at a strict 90-degree angle, and slowly rotate legs one by one, holding at the extreme position while gripping the item.

Stretching

Stretching 1. Against the wall, parallel. Without lifting the hand pressed against the wall and without changing the position of the legs, rotate the torso 180 degrees, then switch the position of legs and arms (targeting chest and shoulder joint mobility).

Stretching for Legs: 2 sets for each side, 30 seconds with a pause at the maximum possible position (targeting the glutes).

Abs and Core

For abs, perform based on sensation, 3 sets (inhale-exhale), for as long as comfortable, preferably no less than 15 times. The right arm moves with the left leg and vice versa. Bring the leg closer to the abdomen to create tension in the abs (with a slight pause).
