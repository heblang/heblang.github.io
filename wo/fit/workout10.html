Fit - Workout 10

STRENGTHENING BACK MUSCLES, LOWER BACK, GLUTES, ABS

3 sets of 15-20 repetitions, slow and concentrated (3-4 seconds per repetition). Between exercises (back - abs), perform light stretching.

First Exercise: Head, shoulders, and arms pressed to the floor, legs bent at a 90-degree angle, and buttocks lifted. Perform alternately without changing the position of the pelvis and without lifting the shoulders, head, and arms.

Second Exercise: Use two chairs; lie on one with legs bent and resting on the floor, use the other as support by holding it with your hands. Lift both legs simultaneously, fully extending them.

Stretching

Stretching 1. 2 sets on each side, 30 seconds with a pause at the maximum position (stretching the lumbar, front, and back of the thigh).

Abs and Core

For abs, perform based on sensation, 3 sets (exhale at the top), for as long as comfortable, preferably no less than 15 times. When returning legs to a horizontal position, touch the floor and slightly spread the legs.
