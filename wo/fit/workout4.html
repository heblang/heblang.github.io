Fit - Workout 4

STRENGTHENING BACK MUSCLES, LOWER BACK, ABS + STRETCHING

3 sets of 15-20 repetitions, slow and concentrated (3-4 seconds per repetition). Between exercises (back - abs), perform light stretching. At the end of each workout, make sure to stretch.

During back exercises, lift the torso slightly above the surface to create tension in the back muscles.

In the first exercise, let the hands slide along the sides of the legs. In the second exercise, it's crucial to keep the arms at a 90-degree angle in the elbows.

Stretching

Stretching 1 - Interlock the fingers and reach for the toes during the incline (an easier variation is to keep the arms straight, reaching forward as far as possible). Repeat 15-20 times (targeting the back of the thigh and calf muscles).

Stretching 2 - Assume the starting position on one side and apply gentle tension with a 15-20 second hold (targeting the chest muscles and upper shoulder area).

Abs and Core

For abs, perform based on sensation, 3 sets (exhale at the top), for as long as comfortable, preferably no less than 15 times. Avoid lifting the neck strongly towards the chest; let the hands slide along the legs. A more challenging variation is to keep the arms in front.
