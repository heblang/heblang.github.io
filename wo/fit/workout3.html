At the end of each workout, make sure to stretch. Abs exercises performed statically while lying on your back are among the safest options.

Stretching

Stretching 1 - 1 set for each leg, 15-20 repetitions, with a slight pause in each repetition (targeting the glutes).

Stretching 2 - 2 sets for each side, 30 seconds with a pause at the end position (targeting the iliacus muscles, front and back of the hamstrings).

Abs and Core

For abs, perform based on sensation, 3 sets (exhale at the top), for as long as comfortable, preferably no less than 15 times. When returning legs to the horizontal position, do not let the heels touch the floor.
