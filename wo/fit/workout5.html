Fit - Workout 5

STRENGTHENING BACK MUSCLES, LOWER BACK, FRONT AND BACK OF THE HAMSTRINGS, GLUTES

3 sets of 15-20 repetitions, slow and concentrated (3-4 seconds per repetition). Between exercises (back - abs), perform light stretching. At the end of each workout, make sure to stretch.

Note that in the first exercise, there are two difficulty variations in one video. The first involves using hands as support during back contraction (preferable for beginners). The second variation requires keeping the hands in the same position but lifting them off the floor, keeping the shoulder blades together.

Stretching

Stretching 1 - 1 set for each leg, 15-20 repetitions, with a slight pause in each repetition (targeting the gluteus medius).

Stretching 2 - In the abs exercise, there is a more challenging variation where the arms are crossed in front of the torso or palms are pressed together in the center of the chest.

Abs and Core

Abs exercises performed statically while lying on your back are among the safest options. Perform abs exercises based on sensation, 3 sets (inhale-exhale), for as long as comfortable, preferably no less than 15 times.
