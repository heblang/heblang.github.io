Fit - Workout 1

MUSCLE STRENGTHENING: BACK, ABS, HAMSTRINGS

3 sets of 15-20 repetitions, slow and concentrated (3-4 seconds per repetition). Between exercises (back - abs), perform light stretching. At the end of each workout, make sure to stretch.

Stretching

Stretching 1. 1 - 2 sets for each leg, one leg bent under you, the other stretched out, hands behind your back, and lean forward (targeting the glutes).

Stretching 2. Apply gentle tension, pause with the movement. Exercise duration is 30 seconds (chest, lower back).

Abs and Core

Abs exercises performed statically while lying on your back are among the safest options. Perform abs exercises based on sensation, 3 sets (e.g., inhale-exhale once, inhale-exhale twice, and so on, for as long as comfortable, preferably no less than 15 times).
